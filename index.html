About Urban-G

Urban-G is a pioneering company that revolutionizes real estate management by partnering with Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs). Each DAO owns a building and its corresponding metaverse, and investors hold DAO tokens proportionally to their investment.

Urban-G's contracts with each DAO ensure continuous management as long as the DAO remains the building owner, creating a symbiotic relationship between investors and the property.

With a groundbreaking format, Urban-G combines tokenization and accommodation models to enhance the living experience and benefits for investors, offering an opportunity for everyone to become a real homeowner regardless of financial status.

Why Choose Urban-G?

Urban-G aims to bridge the gap in traditional real estate ownership, making investors feel like real owners. Alongside economic and tangible owner rights, investors receive various benefits such as discounts, referral codes, free services, and more.

The company envisions the future of hospitality by offering a new, safe, legal, and innovative path that complements the evolving nature of lodging services and overcomes challenges faced by older sharing models due to restrictive city laws and condominium policies.

By combining real estate tokenization and hospitality, Urban-G creates a perfect blend that benefits both investors and tenants, promising a game-changing impact in the market.

Urban-G Tokens

Urban-G has two types of tokens:

  • DAO Tokens: Represent ownership in the DAO, which is the sole proprietor of the building and its associated metaverse space. These tokens are not tradable on secondary markets and can only be bought and sold through the Urban-G platform to comply with US securities laws.
  • SUB Tokens: Complimentary tokens provided to investors upon purchasing the main DAO token. They offer discounted payment for Urban-G services and priority access to future investments. SUB tokens are expected to become a versatile currency usable in various accommodation contexts through partnerships with industry operators.

The tokenomics involve the acquisition of opportunities and assets by the DAO, with investors purchasing DAO tokens to enter a DAO. The minting of SUB tokens occurs at a 1:1 ratio, with each DAO token associated with a SUB token.

Investment Performance

Urban-G's revenue model includes various revenue streams like buying and selling buildings, rental income, sale of services, and projected appreciation. The investment performance is measured through the internal rate of return (IRR), projected to be positive over periods of 5, 10, 20, and 30 years based on existing properties and expected enhancements from Urban-G's business model.
